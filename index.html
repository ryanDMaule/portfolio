ABOUT

ME

I am a mobile developer based in Wales, with a passion for creating intuitive and efficient applications. I specialize in building Android apps, focusing on user experience and clean, maintainable code.

I studied Computer Science at the University of East Anglia for four years, followed by two years as a software engineer. My specialty is mobile development, with strong expertise in Kotlin and adaptable programming skills that enable me to work effectively in multiple languages.

I have never formally studied graphic design, but I’ve always appreciated well-crafted visuals. To refine my skills, I completed the Google UX course on Coursera, and I apply these design principles in every project—some of which you can explore below.
